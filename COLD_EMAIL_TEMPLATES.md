## General Principles

### DO:
- ✅ Research recipient before sending
- ✅ Personalize every email (mention their work)
- ✅ Keep it short (under 150 words)
- ✅ Make it about THEM, not you
- ✅ One clear call to action
- ✅ Follow up 2-3 times if no response
- ✅ Send Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm their timezone

### DON'T:
- ❌ Use generic templates without personalization
- ❌ Send attachments in first email
- ❌ Make it all about you/your product
- ❌ Use manipulative subject lines
- ❌ Send to generic info@ emails
- ❌ Give up after one email

### Email Structure
```
Subject: [Personalized, specific, valuable]
Greeting: [Use their name]
Hook: [Why you're reaching out - about THEM]
Value Prop: [What's in it for them]
CTA: [One clear ask]
Sign-off: [Professional, brief]
```

**Subject:** Should I stay or should I go?

**Body:**

## Email Tracking & Tools

### Recommended Tools

**Free:**
- Mailchimp (email marketing, up to 500 contacts)
- Mailtrack (Gmail tracking)
- Streak CRM (Gmail integration)
- HubSpot Free CRM

**Paid:**
- Hunter.io (find email addresses)
- Reply.io (cold email automation)
- Woodpecker (email outreach)
- Lemlist (personalized cold email)
- Mixmax (email tracking & automation)

### Metrics to Track

- Open rate (aim for 40-50% for cold emails)
- Reply rate (aim for 10-20%)
- Conversion rate (reply → action)
- Best performing subject lines
- Best send times
- Follow-up effectiveness

### Subject Line Testing

**Good subject lines:**
- Personalized: "Loved your article on [topic], [Name]"
- Specific: "Quick question about your [specific thing]"
- Intriguing: "This reminded me of your [work]"
- Direct: "Guest post idea for [Publication]"

**Bad subject lines:**
- Generic: "Collaboration opportunity"
- Salesy: "You need to try this!"
- All caps: "AMAZING PRODUCT"
- Clickbait: "You won't believe this..."

## Legal & Ethics

### DO:
- ✅ Respect unsubscribe requests immediately
- ✅ Include unsubscribe link (if bulk email)
- ✅ Be honest about your product
- ✅ Follow CAN-SPAM Act (US) and GDPR (EU)

### DON'T:
- ❌ Buy email lists
- ❌ Scrape emails without permission
- ❌ Send unsolicited emails to personal addresses
- ❌ Lie or mislead in subject lines
- ❌ Continue emailing after "not interested"

## Template Customization Guide

Before sending ANY email:
1. Replace [placeholders] with actual info
2. Research recipient (5-10 min)
3. Add 1-2 specific references to their work
4. Adjust tone to match their style
5. Proofread (read out loud)
6. Send test to yourself first

**Remember:** Generic emails get ignored. Personalized emails get responses.
